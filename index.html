Grade 1 English Learning

Fun and engaging examples for young learners!

1. Y Sounds Like

  • /e/: Examples: happy, baby, candy
  • /y/: Examples: yellow, yes, yogurt
  • /i/: Examples: gym, myth, crystal

2. C Sounds Like

  • /s/ (soft): Examples: city, center, cycle
  • /k/ (hard): Examples: cat, corn, cup

3. G Sounds Like

  • /j/ (soft): Examples: gem, giant, gym
  • /g/ (hard): Examples: goat, game, gum

4. Linking Verbs

  • Examples: was, were, is, am

5. Naming Words

  • Examples: dog, school, apple, teacher

6. Action Words

  • Examples: sit, run, hid, nap, jump, play, eat, sleep

7. Singular & Plural Nouns

  • Singular: cat, dog, apple
  • Plural: cats, dogs, apples

8. Short & Long Vowel Examples

  • A: Short: cat, bat | Long: cake, lake
  • E: Short: bed, red | Long: tree, bee
  • I: Short: sit, pin | Long: kite, bike
  • O: Short: dog, pot | Long: rope, nose
  • U: Short: sun, cup | Long: cube, mule

9. Consonant Blends

  • S Blends: Examples: stop, swim, slide
  • L Blends: Examples: blue, clap, flag
  • R Blends: Examples: frog, drum, tree

10. Consonant Pairs

  • CH: Examples: chair, cheese, church
  • SH: Examples: ship, shoe, fish
  • TH: Examples: this, that, bath
  • WH: Examples: what, when, where
  • NG: Examples: sing, ring, king

11. Consonant Blends

  • BL: Examples: blue, black, blink
  • CL: Examples: clap, clock, clean
  • FL: Examples: flag, flip, flower
  • GL: Examples: glow, glass, glue
  • PL: Examples: play, plane, plum
  • SL: Examples: slide, slip, slow

12. More Consonant Blends

  • BR: Examples: brown, bread, brush
  • CR: Examples: crab, crown, cry
  • DR: Examples: drum, drop, drive
  • FR: Examples: frog, fruit, frame
  • GR: Examples: green, grape, grow
  • PR: Examples: print, prize, proud
  • TR: Examples: tree, train, truck
  • SC: Examples: scarf, school, score

13. T Blends

  • CT: Examples: act, fact, insect
  • LT: Examples: belt, melt, salt
  • FT: Examples: gift, lift, soft
  • NT: Examples: ant, plant, paint
  • PT: Examples: accept, adapt, concept
  • ST: Examples: best, fast, list
  • XT: Examples: next, text, context
